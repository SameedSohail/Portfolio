About Me

Dr. Humayun Salahuddin

Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Computing and Innovation at Riphah International University, Sahiwal Campus.

  • Specialization: Control Systems, AI, and Machine Learning
  • Current Role: Head of the Department, overseeing academic programs, curriculum, and faculty development.
  • PhD: Control Systems, HITEC University
  • Research Focus: AI, IoT, Electric Vehicles, Biomedical Informatics
  • Key Responsibilities:
    • Developing and accrediting academic programs
    • Supervising faculty and mentoring students
    • Facilitating research and maintaining departmental resources
  • Publications: Multiple papers in journals and conferences on AI, IoT, and automation
  • Funded Projects: Involvement in several funded research projects related to electric vehicle design, AI, and automation
